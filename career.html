Bookkeeper

Are you organized, detail-oriented, and experienced in bookkeeping? Join Urel VA Services as our next Bookkeeper!

Key Responsibilities:

  • Manage accounts payable/receivable.
  • Use Xero (or similar accounting software) to manage invoices, bills, and receipts
  • Ensure accurate transaction, categorization, and reconciliation
  • Lodge BAS (Business Activity Statements) accurately and on time
  • Assist with financial reporting and maintain organized records

Key Skills Required:

  • Proficiency in Xero or equivalent software
  • Strong attention to detail and organizational skills
  • Knowledge of BAS requirements and experience in lodging
  • Ability to work with confidential information
  • Excellent time management and ability to meet deadlines
