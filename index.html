Meet the CEO:
Unica Hermarie "Nicky" Molinyawe

In 2017, Unica took a leap of faith to leave her corporate career and venture into the world of freelance virtual assistance. Fueled by a desire to help businesses grow and provide meaningful support, she quickly discovered her passion for the industry.

During the 2020 pandemic, as her business began to grow, she recognized an even greater opportunity. Not only could she address her own needs, but she could also support others who were struggling with unemployment. This realization led to the founding of UREL VA Services—a company designed to empower aspiring virtual assistants and offer businesses the reliable support they need to succeed.

By 2022, UREL VA Services had expanded globally, providing top-tier virtual assistance to clients worldwide. Unica’s journey has been one of constant evolution, and she is driven by the mission of empowering others to thrive in the virtual assistant industry.
