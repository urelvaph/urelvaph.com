UREL VA Services: Matching You with the Perfect Virtual Assistant

At UREL VA Services, we don’t just provide virtual assistants—we create lasting partnerships by matching you with a professional who fully understands your business needs, goals, and culture. Through our unique process, we ensure that your VA isn’t just qualified but is also a natural fit for your work style, enabling your business to thrive.

How We Make It Happen

In-Depth Needs Discovery

We begin by immersing ourselves in your business to truly understand your unique challenges and objectives. From identifying specific tasks—be it administration, customer support, or marketing—to crafting a tailored Job Description, we lay a solid foundation for finding the perfect VA who aligns with your exact needs.

Tailored Match: Skills & Personality

Your VA is more than a set of skills—they’re an extension of your team. We conduct a comprehensive hiring process to assess not just technical expertise but also attitude, integrity, and alignment with your company culture. Through real-world scenarios and behavioral evaluations, we ensure your VA embodies professionalism, reliability, and a proactive mindset.

Clear Communication & Onboarding

From the outset, we establish mutual trust by aligning on expectations, work hours, and payment terms. Our seamless onboarding process ensures your VA integrates with your team smoothly. With a 90-day structured onboarding plan, we set the stage for successful and long-term collaboration.

Full Support & Continuous Development

We don’t just stop at hiring—we’re committed to your success. Both you and your VA benefit from ongoing support, with regular check-ins at 30, 60, and 90 days, plus annual reviews to ensure everything runs smoothly. We provide continuous development opportunities for your VA, ensuring they remain an asset to your business.
