Our Journey: Empowering People and Businesses Through Virtual Assistance

UREL VA Services was born out of a unique combination of opportunity and empathy during the global pandemic. As the CEO faced an overwhelming surge of work, it became evident that the growing demand for virtual assistance was paired with a significant increase in unemployment, particularly in their home country. This challenging time sparked an idea—one that would not only address the CEO's own business needs but also provide meaningful opportunities to those struggling to find work.

Together with the COO, the vision took shape: a platform dedicated to helping aspiring virtual assistants build careers in a rapidly expanding industry. The duo realized that by offering training, support, and placement, they could empower individuals to create stable, fulfilling careers from the comfort of their homes.

Thus, UREL VA Services was founded—not just to provide excellent virtual assistance to businesses, but to create a pathway for job seekers to thrive in the digital economy. From its humble beginnings, the company has grown into a trusted partner for businesses and virtual assistants alike, committed to excellence, reliability, and long-term relationships.

Mission

To broaden opportunities for aspiring virtual assistants of all ages, providing them with a platform to grow and excel. We are dedicated to being the go-to and reliable VA agency for companies, fostering long-term partnerships rooted in integrity, reliability, and trusted results.

Vision

To be a top-notch virtual assistance agency, recognized for excellence, reliability, and innovation. Within the next 10 years, we aim to establish ourselves as a leading agency that opens doors for both clients and virtual assistants, creating meaningful opportunities and lasting impact.
